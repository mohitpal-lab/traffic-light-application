 Case 1 — NORTH‑SOUTH is GREEN

 NORTH‑SOUTH: GREEN  --->  YELLOW
EAST‑WEST  : RED     --->  RED  (no change)



Case 2 — NORTH‑SOUTH is YELLOW(NORTH‑SOUTH is about to stop.after this step NS stops completely,EW starts moving)

NORTH‑SOUTH: YELLOW ---> RED
EAST‑WEST  : RED    ---> GREEN


Case 3 — EAST‑WEST is GREEN

Transition(EW prepares to stop, NS still waiting):

EAST‑WEST  : GREEN  ---> YELLOW
NORTH‑SOUTH: RED    ---> RED (no change)


Case 4 — EAST‑WEST is YELLOW

Transition(EAST‑WEST is about to stop.after this step, EW stops and NS starts moving)
EAST‑WEST  : YELLOW ---> RED
NORTH‑SOUTH: RED    ---> GREEN


final steps --->

NS_LEFT_GREEN – North–South cars turning left can go.
NS_LEFT_YELLOW – North–South left‑turn cars finish and get ready to stop.
NS_STRAIGHT_PERMISSIVE – North–South cars go straight; left turns allowed only when safe.
NS_STRAIGHT_YELLOW – North–South straight cars slow down and prepare to stop.
EW_LEFT_GREEN – East–West cars turning left can go.
EW_LEFT_YELLOW – East–West left‑turn cars finish and get ready to stop.
EW_STRAIGHT_PERMISSIVE – East–West cars go straight; left turns allowed only when safe.
EW_STRAIGHT_YELLOW – East–West straight cars slow down and prepare to stop.
